HOBBIES

Some of my hobbies are:

The first thing that I love to do aka hobby is to play football. On a regular basis me and my team play futsal. I have loved this since my childhood. 2. Riding Bike

The 2nd thing that I love to do the most is to ride a bike. I love riding bike since 8 grade. I learned it from my father. Every week on saturday or after coming home from college I go for a bike ride with my friends. Riding a bike gives me a completely different and satisfactory feeling of self-love and freedom.
